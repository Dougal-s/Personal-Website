About

Hi, I'm Dougal Stewart (also known as Hunter_Brock) and I am a 16 year old composer and programmer from South Korea.

Programming

I was first inspired to start programming when I was 10, making simple games using php with the help of my dad. At the time I was using programming as a tool to make games. However, once I started diving deeper into computer science, learning how the various languages I was using worked behind the scenes, my fascination with computer science started to grow. Since then, both my interests and skills have grown considerably after working on projects such as Vkav, Penguin and my Non-realtime Audio Processor.

Music

Ironically, music used to be one of my least favourite subjects and was one that I strongly disliked. However, in 2017, I started to find and explore music that I genuinely enjoyed listening to. Eventually, this inspired me to start making music myself and in the summer of 2018, I started creating my first tracks. I immediately found creating music to be gratifying and extraordinarily rewarding despite my apparent lack of talent. Since then, I have been continously producing music for nearly 2 years, learning all I can about music production.
